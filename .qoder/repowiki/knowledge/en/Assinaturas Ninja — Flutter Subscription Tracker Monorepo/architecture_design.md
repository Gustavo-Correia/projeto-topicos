The repository is a standard Flutter project rooted at the workspace root: `pubspec.yaml` declares all shared Dart dependencies (Provider, Hive, intl, uuid, http) consumed by the Flutter app (`lib/`) and its test suite (`test/`). The `android/` and `ios/` directories are the native embedding shells generated by Flutter; they do not contain business logic and exist solely to launch the same Dart entry point. `docs/` holds architecture, UI, validation, and task references that both developers and AI agents consult during development. There is no cross-child wiring beyond the single `flutter run` / `flutter build` workflow that boots the Flutter engine inside each platform shell.
